Spinach Frittata

Spinach frittata

This recipe comes from the book How to Cook Everything: the basics by Mark Bittman. This spinach frittata is a simple, cheap, and relatively healthy breakfast that can be prepared in only 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
  • salt & freshly ground pepper
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 small red onion, chopped
  • 1 pound fresh spinach, chopped
